Principles · do and don't

Do

  • Editorial whitespace — trust the silence.
  • Layered transparency at 60–85%.
  • Thai and Latin as equals.
  • One display size per screen.
  • Confident stops in motion.

Don't

  • Gradient backgrounds.
  • Decorative illustration, stock photo.
  • Excessive accent color — under 5% total.
  • Pure white surfaces.
  • Bouncy or elastic motion. Emoji in UI.
